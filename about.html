About Us


At Eco Jess, we are a passionate and dedicated team of students from the Singapore Polytechnic School Club Partner Program.
Together, "Eco Jess" represents the harmonious relationship between humans and nature, where we strive to cultivate a sustainable and blossoming environment.

Our Mission and Goals

Promoting Sustainable Goals

We believe in the power of education and awareness to drive positive change. Through workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns, we strive to educate the community about sustainable practices and their importance in preserving the planet.

Fostering Eco-Friendly Habits

We understand that small actions can make a big difference. From reducing plastic waste to adopting energy-efficient practices, we encourage people to incorporate eco-friendly habits into their daily lives, fostering a more sustainable society.

Inspiring Volunteerism

Our team firmly believes that collective action can bring about significant change. By organizing volunteering activities and eco-conscious initiatives, we aim to inspire individuals to take an active role in environmental conservation.
